# Surface Trace Catalog

Use this catalog after the facts, argument, scene, and register are already correct. It catches residual patterns that make otherwise usable Chinese feel generated.

Do not let this catalog override genre rules. A strategy deck may use strategy terms, a report may stay neutral, and a personal essay may use first person. The question is whether the choice fits the artifact, not whether it appears on a blacklist.

## 1. Severity And Order

Review in this order:

1. **Evidence risk**: unsupported authority, invented facts, or claims promoted beyond the source.
2. **Meaning risk**: inflated significance, fake causality, false scope, or empty interpretation.
3. **Language risk**: unnatural syntax, synonym cycling, overdesigned phrasing, or repeated templates.
4. **Presentation risk**: excessive bold, emoji, em dashes, inline mini-headings, or chat residue.

Do not spend time removing punctuation while a sentence still makes an unsupported claim.

## 2. Inflated Significance

AI-written text often turns a small fact into evidence of an era, trend, legacy, transformation, or broader social meaning.

High-risk signals:

- 标志着重要转变
- 成为时代缩影
- 彰显其深远意义
- 反映更广泛趋势
- 奠定坚实基础
- 留下不可磨灭的印记
- 在不断演变的格局中发挥关键作用

Test:

- Does the source actually establish the larger trend?
- Would the paragraph lose factual information if the significance sentence were deleted?
- Is the sentence explaining why the writer included the fact rather than telling the reader what happened?

Prefer the specific responsibility, change, consequence, or documented relationship. If the broader significance is important, state the evidence that supports it.

## 3. Vague Attribution

Fail claims that borrow authority without identifying a source or evidence class:

- 行业报告显示
- 有研究表明
- 专家认为
- 业内人士指出
- 多方观察认为
- 相关数据显示

Valid options:

- Name the source, date, sample, or document.
- Downgrade the claim to an inference.
- State that the source is unavailable.
- Delete the borrowed authority and make a narrower claim.

Do not replace a missing source with a more confident sentence.

Media names and follower counts are also weak evidence when the draft never says what was reported, argued, or measured.

## 4. Tail-End Pseudo-Analysis

Chinese AI writing often finishes a factual sentence, then adds a second clause that manufactures importance:

- 从而确保……
- 进而推动……
- 进一步彰显……
- 这也体现了……
- 这也意味着……
- 为……奠定基础
- 有效促进……

Test the tail separately:

1. Does it add a new fact, mechanism, consequence, or decision?
2. Can the writer prove the causal link?
3. Does deleting it make the sentence clearer without losing information?

If the tail only labels the fact as meaningful, delete it. If it names a real consequence, write the actor and causal step.

## 5. Copula Avoidance

AI writing often avoids simple constructions such as `是、有、包括、位于`, replacing them with inflated verbs:

- 作为……的载体
- 充当……的重要空间
- 拥有……的能力
- 设有……的体系
- 提供……的可能性
- 代表着……

Use the simplest accurate structure:

- `A 是 B`
- `A 有 B`
- `A 包括 B`
- `A 位于 B`
- `A 可以做 B`

Keep the longer construction only when the relationship itself matters.

## 6. Synonym Cycling

Do not rotate labels merely to avoid repetition:

- 项目 → 计划 → 方案 → 机制 → 体系
- 用户 → 消费者 → 客群 → 人群 → 受众
- 公司 → 企业 → 品牌 → 组织

When these words refer to the same object, choose one stable term. Change the word only when the referent or analytical level actually changes.

Check titles, table headers, captions, and summary paragraphs together. Terminology drift often appears across surfaces rather than inside one paragraph.

## 7. False Scope And Forced Completeness

Review `从 A 到 B` when A and B do not form a meaningful scale, process, time range, or category boundary.

Also review:

- lists of three created only to sound complete
- headings that claim full coverage without showing the covered set
- `全链路、全场景、全周期、全域` without an explicit boundary

Do not mechanically reduce every three-item list to two items. Keep the number of items the material actually supports.

## 8. Formulaic Outlook And Generic Endings

High-risk section shapes:

- 挑战与未来展望
- 尽管面临挑战，未来依然可期
- 这只是一个开始
- 随着持续推进，将释放更大价值
- 迈出了坚实一步

An ending should perform the artifact's real closing function:

- report: state the unresolved question, implication, or decision
- proposal: identify the next decision, prerequisite, or scope
- article: finish the argument without restating the title
- case: state what the evidence supports and what it does not
- script or narrative: end on the last meaningful change, reaction, image, or question

## 9. Collaboration Residue And Disclaimers

Remove assistant-to-user language that was accidentally left inside the artifact:

- 当然可以
- 希望这对你有帮助
- 如果你需要，我还可以……
- 请告诉我是否需要调整
- 你说得完全正确

Remove model disclaimers unless they are part of the requested audit:

- 根据我最后的训练数据
- 截至我的知识更新时间
- 基于现有有限信息

When information is incomplete, name the missing source, date, field, or assumption directly.

## 10. Formatting Traces

Presentation patterns are low-severity signals, not automatic failures:

- every bullet starts with a bold mini-heading and colon
- repeated emoji headings
- multiple em dashes used as a substitute for sentence structure
- ordinary words placed in quotation marks for false emphasis
- every paragraph ends with a standalone slogan
- every section has the same number of bullets and same visual rhythm

Keep formatting when it supports scanning or matches the user's template. Remove it when it creates artificial hierarchy without information value.

## 11. Reader-Trust Failures

Do not explain the same point at three levels:

1. state the fact
2. interpret the fact
3. restate the interpretation as a takeaway

Delete one layer and check whether the reader still understands. Keep explanation only when it resolves ambiguity, supplies causality, or changes the decision.

Also challenge sentences that explain an obvious metaphor, repeat a chart title in prose, or summarize a table without adding a comparison or judgment.

## 12. Voice Vacuum After Cleanup

Removing templates can leave correct but sterile text. Before adding personality, identify what the scene permits:

- Which facts does the writer choose to emphasize?
- What judgment, uncertainty, tradeoff, or consequence matters?
- What sentence rhythm fits this channel?
- Does the artifact need a personal voice, an editorial voice, or neutral analysis?

Personality comes from selection, stance, and rhythm. It does not require first person, humor, slang, anecdotes, or deliberate disorder.

## 13. Rules Not To Import Mechanically

- Do not invent dates, numbers, features, quotes, reactions, or personal stories to replace vague text.
- Do not add `我` merely to sound human.
- Do not force humor, messiness, digressions, or emotional ambivalence into professional material.
- Do not ban every three-part list, em dash, bold phrase, slogan, or quotable sentence.
- Do not use a numerical "human score" as proof that the writing is finished.
- Do not apply English-only signals such as title capitalization or `-ing` grammar without a real Chinese equivalent.

## Final Gate

After the surface pass, confirm:

- No fact or evidence level changed.
- The register still fits the scene.
- The writer's terminology remains stable.
- No significance, causality, or authority was added without support.
- Cleanup did not make the text uniformly dry or force every genre into the same voice.
